About

Background

I've always wanted to broaden my scope in data — to work with problems that are bigger, messier, and more consequential. That drive led me to financial services, where the data is richer, the stakes are higher, and the questions worth answering are harder.

At Bank SinoPac — one of Taiwan's top 5 private banks — I moved from analyst to engineer not by accident, but by intent. I saw that AI could fundamentally change how teams interact with data, and in early 2025 I built the company's first internal Text-to-SQL agent from scratch. That felt like the right direction to keep pulling.

Before banking, I spent over a year at Gamania Digital Entertainment — Taiwan's largest online gaming company — developing a foundation in behavioral analytics, A/B experimentation, and large-scale SQL across millions of active users.

Now I'm at USC, deepening the theory behind the practice.
